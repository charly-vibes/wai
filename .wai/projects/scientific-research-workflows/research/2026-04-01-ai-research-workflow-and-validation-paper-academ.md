AI Research Workflow and Validation paper - academic synthesis using Anthropic's 'vibe physics' experiment as case study. Argues for structured validation frameworks to mitigate AI failure modes (context drift, sycophancy, hallucinated data smoothing) in scientific research. Maps wai tools to solutions: design-practice for premature optimization, Rule of 5 for intermediate auditing, multi-agent-review for scaling validation. Identifies gaps: cryptographic provenance, deterministic pipeline halting on validation failure, disagreement-trigger HITL mode, DataFlow-style operator pipelines. Three action tracks identified: (1) feature roadmap for wai, (2) pipeline formula for scientific users, (3) validation/marketing material for existing toolset.
